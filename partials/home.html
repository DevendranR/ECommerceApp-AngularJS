Who We Are

FiveY Ventures is a division of Sridhans Consulting based in Hosur and Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. We are a small super-charged team with expertise in Industrial Consulting, Private Relations, B2B marketing and hospitality background.

Our goal is simple- to ensure high quality construction needs under one roof with high customer satisfaction. While our vision is noble, we back up that vision with reliable, trustworthy, professional service and high-quality industry competence. Our passion translates into positive working relationship and transparency that reflects in our true secret for success and business relationships.

We provide high quality construction products like Cements, TMT Steel bars, M-Sand, UPVC windows directly from respected brands. We make sure you get exactly what you want. We then follow up with you for other requirements in every stage of your construction. We also offer Insurance across various segments which includes Industrial, Marine, travel, health, Workmen compensation, Professional indemnity, Public liability and automobiles.
